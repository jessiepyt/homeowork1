Myths behind Hong Kong's status as world's freest economy under threat

by Pang Yu Tung Jessie

Hong Kong has topped economic freedom index for 22 years. Yet, different news organizations always claim Hong Kong's status as world's freest economy is being threat by various factors and neighbouring countries. Is it the truth? Let's find out the truth behind these myths:
